Characteristics

Here you can define the characteristics of your system.

Characteristics are mostly computed values based on attributes.

Example: "Hit points"

${E} * 20 + 20

What does it mean

${E} * 20 + 20 : Hit points are equal to the value of the attribute Endurance ${E}, multiplied by 20, plus 20.

So if the character has an Endurance of 5, the hit points will be 120.

Note that if the character has an Endurance of 0, the hit points will be 20.
