Perks

With perks, you can modify almost everything in a character.

Exemple: "Ninja perk"

    ${CAR_INITIATIVE} += 5
    ${CAR_DODGE} += 5
    ${SKILL_FIGHT} += 15
    ${SKILL_SILENT_WALK} += 10
    ${SKILL_THROWING} += 10

What does it mean

  • ${CAR_INITIATIVE} += 5 : The character will have 5 more points in the initiative characteristic.
  • ${CAR_DODGE} += 5 : The character will have 5 more points in the dodge characteristic.
  • ${SKILL_FIGHT} += 15 : The character will have 15 more points in the fight skill.
  • ${SKILL_SILENT_WALK} += 10 : The character will have 10 more points in the silent walk skill.
  • ${SKILL_THROWING} += 10 : The character will have 10 more points in the throwing skill.

As you can see, with a single perk, you can tune a character in many ways.
